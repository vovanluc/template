Essential Slap Bass Rudiments with Phil Mann: Volume 2


Course available to Academy members only

Course Overview:

If you’re interested in really getting into some complex slap bass rudiments then this is the course for you!

In this course Phil returns for the second instalment in his slap bass series! Phil will be taking an in-depth look at some more advanced 16th slap bass patterns. Throughout this course you’re going to really gain an insight into how to develop intricate rudiments and how to play them in an effortless way. You’ll be taking a look at everything from Victor Wooten’s Open-Hammer-Pluck playing to Marcus Millers double pluck melodies!

In This Course You’re Going to Learn About:

  • Slapping 16th patterns like Mark King!
  • Building accuracy, stamina & efficiency with your technique
  • Left & right hand rudiments
  • Incorporating hammer-ons in your slap playing
  • How to “Open-Hammer-Pluck” like Victor Wooten!
  • And WAY more…

Ready to get in the shed? Let’s jump in!
