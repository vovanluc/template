Frequently asked questions:

I am a complete beginner. Will the SBL Academy be right for me?

Yes! We have a specific course for beginners that will take you through 'exactly' what you need to know to get going on your new bass. But more than that, here at SBL Academy we have "The Campus" - the largest bass educational forum in the world. Our members are a true inspiration in the way they help and guide people just like you. You will have somewhere to ask questions, get feedback and direction 24hrs a day.

I am an intermediate to advanced bass player. Will the SBL Academy be right for me?

Yes! Every month Scott regularly covers material that is for the more intermediate to advanced bass player. So if you want to really challenge yourself and take your bass playing to the next level you're in the right place. Get ready, Scott is going to get you working harder than you've ever done before.

I'm a professional bass player and teaching my own students. Is the Academy right for me?

Yes! In fact our members already include bass players that have taught or are teaching right now in Universities and music schools around the world. Many of our members are actually using Scott's teaching philosophies with their own students so we invite you to do the same!

Can I ask Scott questions directly?

Yes. Every other week we have a completely live Q&A masterclass with Scott. You can pre-submit your questions or even ask him your questions live. Plus, if you miss the live Q&A each one "Bass-Hang" (that's what we call them!) is recorded and then uploaded into the Bass-hang archive so you can watch in your own time.

Why should I become a member today instead of waiting?

It's time for you to take action. Simple as that. If you really want to make a difference to your bass playing you need somewhere you get get answers for all your questions. You need support, community, and most of all... accountability! SBL Academy is that place. If you really want to make a difference, the time is now.

What if I don't enjoy the SBL Academy?

We know you will LOVE the SBL Academy as over 4000 bass players around the world have already signed up in the last few months. Can I download the video material within the SBL Academy.

It depends. All the videos within the lesson library are available to download giving you the ability to access them offline at any time. The exclusive video content within the SBL Academy is not available for download.

Can I access the content released at the SBL Academy during my membership once I cancel?

No. Your membership entitles you to enter the members area and access the content there released prior and during the duration of your membership. If you do not renew at the end of your 12 month membership you will no longer be able to access the content inside the SBL Academy. Don't worry though, we will email you and ask whether you want to renew your membership or not, so your membership won't be deleted automatically.
