Harmonic Layering – Arpeggios, Chord Tones & Scales

Watch Course Introduction

Course Overview:

This 10+ hour course is a fully comprehensive guide to arpeggios, chord tones and scales with a focus of cementing their relationships together in a way that will make them instantly usable within any musical situation.

Within this course Scott takes you right from the start, then guides you through a carefully crafted curriculum, step by step.

The course is comprised of four in-depth modules in which Scott shares everything he knows regarding this invaluable subject. In each module he takes you step by step through the process of applying this knowledge directly to your bass, but unlike many other methods and resources, Scott connects all the dots for you.

Within this course you’re going to learn a practical method of creating awesome bass lines and solos in a clear and understandable way, that’s instantly usable.

Simply put… this is a must have resource for every bassist.

8 Core Things you'll learn within this course

1 Scott's method of mapping out the entire fingerboard by using only 3 simple fingering patterns for each scale.

2 How to use arpeggio's, chord tones and scales to create instant bad-ass bass lines in every genre of music.

3 Scott's "Harmonic Layering" technique that solidifies the relationship between the arpeggios and scales in a way that makes them much easier to understand and instantly usable.

4 The continuous scale and arpeggio exercises ( Scott's favorite ever exercises!).

5 How to easily understand the modes, and more importantly - exactly how to use them.

6 Scott shares his favorite uses of more exotic scales such as the Melodic Minor, Harmonic Minor, Whole Tone & Diminished scales. (This part of the course alone is well over an hour long!)

7 How to use pentatonic scales and the blues scale when creating bass lines or soloing.

8 How to approach soloing over one chord vs multiple chords and the different approaches you should take.

And much, much more...
