The Campus

The Campus is the heart of the SBL Academy community and is completely different to every other community you've ever been a member of. Simple as that. Its sole purpose is to act as an interactive learning environment for bass players so you can get tips, advice, ask questions, share inspiration, post up your own videos to get helpful critique, meet like minded bass players and much more!

When you become a member of the SBL Academy you'll get instant access to this thriving community and finally be able to connect with other bass players just like you. Bass players who actually care about their bass playing and are willing to go the extra mile to make it happen.

"I've been playing bass for over 25 years and have played professionally backing number one artists from the 60's and even a stint in a West End show. Since becoming a member of the SBL Academy my bass playing has progressed faster than any other time." - Nathan Hulse

Here are just a few of the things you'll get access to within The Campus

  • Our awesome monthly challenges.
  • The SBL Academy weekly show.
  • Direct feedback and advice about your bass playing from the SBL team and our other rock star members.
  • Access to the SBL "Practice Logs" where all our members can create a personal practice log for themselves for ultimate accountability.
  • Monthly Challenge Workshops.

   And much more!
