Academy Faculty:

Phil Mann

  • Profile
  • Media
  • Seminars

After completing the Basstech Diploma and LCM Degree program in London, Philip secured a Masters Degree in Jazz Performance, before receiving a scholarship to attend Jeff Berlins ‘Players School of Music’ in America.

Phil has appeared in London’s West End on Dancing In the Street, across Europe on productions such as Jesus Christ Super Star, One Night In Vegas, Little Shop Of Horrors, Anything Goes, Fame, Bugsy Malone, The Pirates Of Penzance, Beyond Phantom, Return To The Forbidden Planet, Hairspray, Jack The Ripper, and back home on The National Lottery Live.

His performance credits include working with Big Band Metheny (a 14 piece Pat Metheny big band), opening for Marcus Miller at BassDay UK 2011, playing conventional bass for TM Stevens on his 2012 UK Clinic Tour, and also being featured as an artist at the London Bass Guitar Show in both 2012 and ’13.

Phil laying down some Funk/Jazz at the world famous jazz club Ronnie Scotts!
