We’re hiring! Join us as “Backstage Manager” at SBL…

Posted in: Notes

The SBL team is once again growing! We’re entering into an exciting stage of growth. and this is a great opportunity for the right person to join a very special company.

I am the face of what we do here at SBL and predominately create all of the video content for both marketing and Academy purposes. Right now, even more of my energy is spent 'backstage' managing the many different elements that it takes to steer this exciting company and keep everything running on track so we can go from strength to strength. But - it's time to take what we're doing here to the next level, and this is where you come in!

Your Role: SBL Backstage Manager (Full-Time Position)

As our Backstage Manager you will be working within the core SBL team - in fact, you'll be managing it!

You will be the overall project manager here at SBL. What does this mean? That you’ll be grand central station for all of our projects. You’ll break down our goals into deliverables, hand them off to the right people, then make sure everything is on track and getting done on time. You’re the go-to person for questions, resources, or whatever our team needs to get the job done. You are excellent at translating big ideas into projects, from figuring out the master plan to figuring out exactly which details need to be executed when.

You’ll also be our “virtual office manager,” doing HR types of tasks like paying contractors, and interfacing with our bookkeeper to keep tabs on finances.

Basically, you are the one who keeps our virtual office humming every day. You keep our team happy and our projects on time.

We are a virtual office — that means everyone (including you!) works from your own home office. This means you get all the best parts of interacting with a super fun team, with none of the commute and cubicles! Although your day to day will involve working closely with our small team, you need to be the kind of person that enjoys working from home and managing their own time.

Interested? Let’s do this!…

What's it like working in team SBL?

1. You'll be working with talented individuals that dream BIG!

2. Our team works remotely, so your location won’t hold you back.

3. We're changing online bass education into something that can truly guide people and take their playing to the next level. If you want to be part of something that's changing the world - you're the right fit.

4. All positions start on a self contractor basis. This set-up is the most simple and clean to start with. We’re a team and business that’s growing FAST and we’re up for constantly evaluating where talented people can fill roles and provide value to our community. If you’re a go getter, you’ll succeed here fast.

Some of your tasks will include:

1. Translating big ideas into projects, deliverables, & timelines

2. Setting up processes and making sure everything is running smoothly within each task or process.

3. Working with everyone on our team to determine their top 3 monthly goals,then tracking progress/success on those goals

4. Monitoring our company management system, actively looking for roadblocks and making sure everything is progressing

5. Overseeing all of our sites and working with our team to make sure they’re up to date

6. Managing and overseeing site/content audit's

7. Overseeing course and content creation — working with our team to break down the course, put together the materials, & make it live and functional for customers

8. Facilitating weekly team meetings

Requirements:

1. You have a proven track record of successful previous management role and are a great team builder and team coordinator.

2. Completing tasks on time to an exceptionally high standard is a must for you.

3. You will be able to take loose directions and turn them highly focused projects and plans.

4. You have a logical, structured, organised, and systematised thought process.

5. You are extremely detail oriented.

6. Tech – savvy (meaning you don’t mind getting your hands dirty learning some web development, design etc. when necessary). Basic technical knowledge is a not required, but definitely a plus!

7. You don’t make ANY assumptions, and always ask questions to gain more clarity.

8. Ideally you will have copy writing skills and understand that SBL has a ‘voice’ and style that should be captured and conveyed within any written medium.

9. You are INSANELY resourceful - spotting hidden opportunities for growth is second nature for you.

10. You are CRAZY smart.

11. You need to have fun!

More About You:

You are outrageously organised — colour-coding, folders, and systems are the stuff of dreams. Taking an idea and turning it into a timeline of clear deliverables actually sounds like a fun way to spend a day to you! You are incredibly skilled at both micro and macro thinking — making sure the big picture is always moving forward by way of all the tiny details that need to get done.

You also love people — you’re a natural mentor and sounding board, and knowing that you helped someone else succeed gives you enormous satisfaction. You love coaching people to do their best, seeing people’s strengths and talents and draw- ing it out of them even more. You naturally pick up on subtle team dynamics and are able to proactively jump in to assuage a concern or just offer a few words of encouragement. You are an incredible problem solver — you are strategic, clever, and quick on your feet. You’ll be the go-to person when we’re trying to figure out a sticky issue.

You are extremely resourceful and great at playing detective — you can unravel any problem and figure out what’s really going on, what the missing pieces are, and go out and find them.

You do NOT thrive on being micromanaged and love to be given the freedom to excel in your job. Being your own boss within an organisation is a dream come true for you.

You are open, direct, and straightforward. You believe that honesty, awareness, and ownership are the direct routes to problem solving. You’d rather not play the “blame game,” but instead just correct the issue and keep moving! You are a solution-based thinker who investigates why things went wrong with the goal of improving the process. You believe that a process problem lies at the root of every apparent people problem.

It is pretty impossible to faze you and you’re able to take a hit and keep rolling — your goal is to get it done, even if the path that gets there isn’t quite the one that you anticipated!

Apply Now:

Your application must include:

1. A résumé and a video cover letter. Record yourself speaking in a 3(ish)-minute video and upload it to YouTube. Highlight your passions and motivations for applying. Tell us how your skills and expertise would contribute to this stellar team. Be specific.

2. Include into your résumé links to projects you've worked on in the past.
